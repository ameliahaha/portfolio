About ---------------------

At Cab Calloway, I strive to enhance my creative skills in digital media, such as: website design, Adobe Creative Cloud, cinematography/photography, etc. Outside of the arts, I also excel in academics as I have a 4.8W GPA. Currently, I tutor young children as a pastime and manage for my Little League's Softball team whilst being a part of the Charter School of Wilmington Softball Program.
